Replace rear toothed belt cover

J463053

  Remove

1.   Remove toothed belt
2.   Remove high pressure pump drive gear
•   Unscrew locking bolt
•   Unscrew nut
Important:   Note disc spring
– Counterhold with KM-6603 (1), rest KM-6603 at position (2) toothed belt drive gear
•   Withdraw high pressure pump drive gear with KM-6602 (4)
– Hold using KM-6603
Note:   Rest KM-6603 at position (3) toothed belt drive gear


3.   Remove toothed belt guide roller (1)
•   Unscrew bolt (2)


4.   Lower vehicle by its full height
5.   Remove camshaft sprocket
•   Remove TDC locking bolt (1)
– Unscrew bolt
Note:   Counterhold with KM-6347 (3) in combination with KM-956-1 (2)


6.   Remove rear toothed belt cover (1)
•   Unscrew 6x bolt (2)


  Install

7.   Install rear toothed belt cover
•   Tighten 6x bolt 10 Nm
8.   Install camshaft sprocket
Note:   Journal (2) camshaft must engage in bore (1) camshaft sprocket
•   Tighten bolt 111 Nm
– Counterhold with KM-6347 in conjunction with KM-956-1
Important:   Do not damage increment disc
•   Screw in TDC locking bolt


9.   Raise vehicle by its full height
10.   Install toothed belt guide roller
•   Tighten bolt 80 Nm
11.   Fit high pressure pump drive gear
Note:   Note disc spring
•   Tighten nut 69 Nm
– Hold using KM-6603
12.   Install toothed belt
