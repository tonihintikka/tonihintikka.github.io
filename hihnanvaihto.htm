Replace toothed belt

J462400

  Remove

1.   Open bonnet
2.   Disconnect battery
•   Detach negative connection from earth terminal
– Undo nut
3.   Remove engine cover
4.   Remove air cleaner housing
5.   Remove air intake pipe (1) with mass air flow meter
•   Release clamp
•   Disconnect mass air flow meter wiring harness plug


6.   Raise vehicle by its full height
7.   Remove engine compartment cover or right hand engine splash guard
8.   Insert KM-6173 (3)
•   Align support bearing (1)
•   Turn support bearing up until journal (2) sits flush in holder (4)
•   Attach KM-6173
– Screw in 2x bolts


9.   Attach KM-6001-A (1)
Note:   Attaching KM-6001-A guarantees perfect alignment of the drive unit with the front axle body
•   Slacken 4x bolts (arrows) in adjusting rails
•   Insert KM-6001-A
– Insert journals (2) and (5) in guide holes in front axle body
•   Tighten 4x bolts in adjusting rails
•   Adjust support bearing at front (4) and rear (3)
– Raise support bearings up to the stop on the guide journals
Note:   The guide journals must be seated free from play in the support bearings


10.   Remove ribbed V-belt
Note:   Mark direction of rotation
•   Tighten ribbed V-belt tensioner via bolt (1) in direction of arrow
•   Secure ribbed V-belt tensioner at bore (2) with KM-6130
•   Remove ribbed-V-belt


11.   Remove torsional vibration damper (1)
•   Unscrew 4x bolts
Note:   Counterhold at bolt (2)


12.   Lower vehicle by its full height
13.   Remove right engine damping block (2)
•   Detach from side member
– Unscrew 3x bolts (1)
•   Detach from engine damping block bracket
– Unscrew 3x bolts (3)


14.   Remove right engine damping block bracket (1)
•   Unscrew 3x bolts


15.   Remove camshaft sensor (1)
•   Disconnect wiring harness connector.
•   Unscrew bolt (2)


16.   Remove upper toothed belt cover (2)
•   Unscrew 8x bolt (arrows)
Note:   Note different bolt lengths
•   Unclip 4x fuel return pipe (1)


17.   Raise vehicle by its full height
18.   Remove lower toothed belt cover (1)
•   Unscrew 3x bolts (2)
•   Remove engine damping block adapter


19.   Set cylinder 1 to TDC of combustion stroke
•   Secure camshaft sprocket with TDC locking bolt (M6x25) (1)
•   Turn crankshaft evenly in direction of engine rotation until marking (3) on toothed belt drive gear aligns with marking (2) on oil pump cover


20.   Slacken the toothed belt tension roller (1)
•   Slacken the toothed belt tension roller bolt (2)
•   Twist toothed belt tension roller approx. 90° counter-clockwise
•   Tighten bolt, toothed belt tension roller


21.   Remove toothed belt
•   Mark direction of rotation

  Install

22.   Install toothed belt
Note:   TDC locking bolt must be installed in camshaft sprocket (9) and high pressure pump wheel (8) and markings (6) and (7) must align
•   Position toothed belt
Note:   Observe direction of rotation
– Toothed belt must be tightened in direction of arrow from toothed belt drive gear (5) via oil pump drive wheel (4) and high pressure pump drive wheel (2) to camshaft sprocket (1).
•   Slacken the toothed belt tension roller (3)
•   Tighten toothed belt tension roller 49 Nm



23.   Timing, Check
•   Remove 2x TDC locking bolts
•   Turn crankshaft 720° in the direction of rotation of the engine
– At toothed belt drive gear
•   marking of toothed belt drive gear must align with casting mark on oil pump cover
•   Screw in 2x TDC locking bolts
Note:   If it is not possible to screw in a TDC locking bolt, the basic adjustment must be repeated.
•   Unscrew 2x TDC locking bolts
24.   Install lower toothed belt cover
•   Insert engine damping block adapter
•   Tighten 3x bolt 10 Nm
25.   Lower vehicle by its full height
26.   Install upper toothed belt cover
•   Tighten 8x bolt 10 Nm
Note:   Note different bolt lengths
•   Clip in 4x fuel return pipe
27.   Install camshaft sensor
•   Replace seal ring
•   Tighten bolt 10 Nm
•   Fix wiring harness plug
28.   Install engine damping block bracket , right side
•   Tighten 3x bolt 60 Nm
29.   Install engine damper block
•   Attach to longitudinal member
– Tighten 3x bolt 35 Nm
•   Attach engine damping block bracket
– Tighten 3x bolt 55 Nm
30.   Raise vehicle by its full height
31.   Install torsional vibration damper
•   Tighten 4x bolt 20 Nm
Note:   Counterhold toothed belt drive gear at bolt
32.   Insert ribbed V-belt
Note:   Observe direction of rotation
•   Position ribbed V-belt
– I. Ribbed V-belt without AC system
– II. Ribbed V-belt with AC system
•   Tighten ribbed V-belt tensioner via bolt (1) in direction of arrow
– Remove KM-6130



33.   Remove engine mount KM-6001-A
34.   Remove engine mount KM-6173
35.   Install lower engine compartment cover or engine splash guard, right side
36.   Lower vehicle by its full height
37.   Install air intake pipe with mass air flow meter
•   Tighten clamp 3.5 Nm
•   Connect mass air flow meter wiring harness plug
38.   Install air cleaner housing
39.   Install engine cover
40.   Connect battery
•   Attach negative connection to earth terminal
– Tighten nut
41.   Program volatile memories
42.   Close bonnet
